## Prompt 1: Kitchen Chef 食品品牌

Create a 3×3 grid of promotional social media posts for a food brand called "Kitchen Chef." Use a modern organic style with a muted sage green, warm cream, orange, and charcoal color palette. Include hand-drawn vegetable line art, playful bold typography, abstract organic shapes, and a chef logo with utensils. Feature fresh sandwiches, wraps, pita, and vegetables held by hands in dynamic close-up shots. Add catchy curved headlines like "Built Different," "Sandwich Therapy Works," "Crunch You Can Hear," "Fresh Ingredients," and "Trust The Crunch," with consistent branding and the contact number 0998 304 445. The overall look should feel premium, fresh, healthy, and creative like a modern fast-casual food campaign.

## Prompt 2: CHAGO 茶果 茶饮品牌

Create a 3×3 grid of premium social media promotional graphics for a specialty tea brand called "CHAGO (茶果)." Use a modern Asian tea-house aesthetic with a soft pastel pink and deep forest green color palette, elegant studio lighting, geometric pedestals, and surreal creative photography. Feature iced teas, milk teas, fruit beverages, boba drinks, and signature flavors with artistic compositions, fresh fruits, bamboo, and minimalist props. Include clean luxury typography, brand-focused layouts, and concepts like Jasmine Milk Tea, Grapefruit Citrus Tea, Coconut Shaken Espresso, Peach Oolong, and Aged Milk Tea. The overall style should feel sophisticated, refreshing, premium, and visually striking like a high-end beverage campaign.
