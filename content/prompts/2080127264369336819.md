Create a highly detailed 3D mixed-media vintage collage, assemblage, decoupage, and paper-craft artwork in a [ASPECT RATIO] format, centered around [MAIN SUBJECT OR CHARACTERS] engaged in [POSE, ACTION, OR NARRATIVE MOMENT].

Render the focal subjects using [PRIMARY ART TECHNIQUE], combining [SECONDARY TECHNIQUE], with visibly layered physical materials, torn paper edges, overlapping cut-outs, tactile surfaces, dimensional embellishments, subtle raised elements, and realistic soft drop shadows that create convincing handcrafted depth.

The subjects should wear [CLOTHING, ERA, AND COLOR PALETTE], with distinctive details such as [HAIR, ACCESSORIES, EXPRESSIONS, OR PROPS]. Integrate [DECORATIVE ELEMENTS] naturally throughout the composition, allowing selected materials to overlap the central imagery and extend beyond frames or paper layers.

Build the background from [BASE MATERIAL], featuring [VINTAGE TEXTURES, HANDWRITING, LEDGER LINES, NEWSprint, MUSICAL NOTES, FLORAL PATTERNS, WATER STAINS, CRACKLES, OR OTHER DETAILS]. Use visibly distressed, weathered, aged surfaces with torn edges, faded ink, sepia discoloration, subtle stains, and authentic antique imperfections.

Frame or structure the central composition with [FRAME, BORDER, OR COLLAGE STRUCTURE], incorporating [FLOWERS, LACE, RIBBONS, BUTTONS, PEARLS, BUTTERFLIES, OR OTHER EMBELLISHMENTS] as tactile physical elements.
Maintain a harmonious palette of [COLOR PALETTE], balancing nostalgic muted tones with [ACCENT COLORS].

Use warm, soft antique ambient lighting with gentle directional highlights and realistic contact shadows beneath every raised material, emphasizing the handcrafted 3D construction.

The overall aesthetic should feel like a precious vintage scrapbook, Victorian storybook artifact, antique postcard, heirloom craft, and museum-quality assemblage artwork, combining romantic nostalgia with sophisticated editorial composition.

NEGATIVE:
No modern digital UI, no plastic appearance, no flat vector graphics, no excessive photorealism, no random text, no illegible typography, no contemporary objects, no visible watermarks.

Cheat Sheet:
[ASPECT RATIO] → 1:1 square
[MAIN SUBJECT OR CHARACTERS] → Victorian children, vintage portraits, paper-doll women, etc.
[POSE / ACTION] → Standing together, holding hands, embracing, walking, posing

[PRIMARY ART TECHNIQUE] → Decoupage, mixed-media collage, paper craft
[SECONDARY TECHNIQUE] → Paper quilling, scrapbooking, assemblage, layered cut-outs

[CLOTHING / ERA / COLORS] → Victorian gowns, vintage trench coats, muted antique palette
[DECORATIVE ELEMENTS] → Lace, ribbons, pearls, buttons, butterflies, flowers

[BASE MATERIAL] → Aged parchment, distressed paper, textured board
[BACKGROUND DETAILS] → Cursive writing, ledger lines, newsprint, stains, cracks

[FRAME / STRUCTURE] → Oval filigree frame, torn photo border, layered paper composition
[COLOR PALETTE] → Sepia, cream, sage, terracotta, blush, charcoal
[ACCENT COLORS] → Choose 1 to 3 signature colors
